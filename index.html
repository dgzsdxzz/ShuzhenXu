Ziyu Wan (万子钰)

Google Scholar | Linkedin | Email | GitHub | Twitter | Blog

I am a senior researcher at Microsoft GenAI.
I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong in 2024, where I was fortunate advised by Prof. Jing Liao.
My current research interests include:

  • Multi-modality (text, image, video, 3D and 4D) generation
  • Computational Photography
  • Neural Rendering
