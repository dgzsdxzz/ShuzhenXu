I am a lecturer at Yantai University.
I received my Ph.D. from the Faculty of Information Technology, Beijing University of Technology in 2020.
My current research interests include:

  • Image Inpainting, Image Super-Resolution, Image Defogging, Image Deraining, Image Denoising and other Image Generation Techniques
  • (Camouflaged, Salient) Object Detection
  • 3D Generation
